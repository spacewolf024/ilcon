About Il Coniglio

We are very excited to bring Il Coniglio to you. Here, you’ll be immersed in a French/Italian amalgam, echoing old world comfort while advancing gastronomic technique and embracing monumental hybrid design. Matchlessly blurring the lines between kitchen and dining room, this interactive yet distinguished approach will uniquely fulfill your desire to be a part of the culinary experience while still feeling like an honored guest. Il Coniglio will showcase Chef Dirk’s state of the art cuisine in a shared environment, creating an untapped culture for casual fine dining.
